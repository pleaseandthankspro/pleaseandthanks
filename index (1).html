About
Please & Thanks Productions was founded by Henry Molofsky and Joel Lovell, formerly of Pineapple Street Studios. The team's past work includes "Wind of Change," with Patrick Radden Keefe, and several shows with Dan Taberski, including "Hysterical," "9/12," "Running from COPS," and "Missing Richard Simmons." Their work has been honored as Apple's Show of the Year and the Ambies' Best Podcast of the Year (twice), and they have been finalists for a Peabody Award and a Pulitzer Prize.
